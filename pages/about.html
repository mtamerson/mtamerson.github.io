About This Project

Hi there! My name is Michael and I come from California, but I currently live in Rwanda with my beautiful and hilarious wife, Angel.

I am finishing three years of volunteering in the Peace Corps, so I have been thinking a lot about my repatriation back to the United States. I started applying for jobs online, but the prospects aren't looking good.

That's why I decided to learn how to code websites. I figure the more skills that I have, the more competitive it makes me in a market where jobs are scarce.

So come along with me as I build this website and see all of the cool things that I am learning!
