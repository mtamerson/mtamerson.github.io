Peace Corps | Rwanda Website

As a Peace Corps Volunteer Leader, I served many roles in Rwanda. One of those was as a Communications Specialist. I helped launch and maintain the very first Peace Corps | Rwanda website. Peace Corps recently changed the way that it recruits new volunteers, so it is important that every Peace Corps post around the world be able to interest applicants in their specific programs.

One of the challenges of creating this website was the need to adhere to a specific template provided by Peace Corps HQ while still trying to find creative ways to draw people to the website. One of the ways we accomplished this was by embedding a background image of Rwanda's landscape that fades in as you scroll down the page. We put a sliding banner and a video on the front page to immediately engage visitors. Lastly, we made plans to update the website with more photos, videos and blog posts to keep people coming back for more.
